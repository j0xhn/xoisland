Nope, we have a couple of different payment options and in-between packages. Request our packet by emailing info@xoisland.com or feel free to give us a call: 208.227.3646. We even have a special one for bloggers!

Where is the Island located

It's off the coast of Nicaragua, close to a town called "Bluefields" in the Caribbean. Here is a link to the google map. To see the exact group of islands you have to download google earth though.

What Does Unlimited Access Imply?

It means that if your package hasn't been reserved two weeks in advance you are free to reserve it for as long as you wish. This comes with the annual membership fee of $90/person (which is discounted with group packages)
